Ad Formats

Native Banners

These ads seamlessly integrate into your site's content, matching the look and feel of your page. They provide a non-intrusive advertising experience, enhancing user engagement while delivering high revenue potential.

Learn More

Native Floaters

Floating ads are designed to appear as overlay elements on your site, providing visibility without disrupting the user’s content experience. They are highly effective in capturing user attention and generating additional revenue.

Learn More

Video Ads

This format includes pre-roll, mid-roll, and post-roll video ads that play before, during, or after video content on your site. Video ads are known for their high engagement rates and can be a powerful tool for maximizing revenue.

Learn More

Reward Ads

Reward ads offer users incentives, such as getting to read your premium articles for free, in exchange for engaging with the ad. This format enhances user experience by providing value and can significantly increase interaction rates.

Learn More

Push Notification Ads

Push notifications allow you to send targeted ads directly to users' devices even when they are not actively on your site. This format helps maintain user engagement and drive traffic, leading to increased revenue opportunities.
